2. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal data when you:

The data we may collect includes:

  • Name, address, email address, phone number
  • Login credentials (username)
  • Payment and billing details
  • Service and property preferences

  • Marketing preferences (e.g., newsletter opt-in)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage your account(s)
  • Provide and improve our services (e.g., property maintenance, pool care, landscaping, repairs)
  • Communicate with you about your account, bookings, or quotes
  • Process payments and billing
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g., job completion, staff login)

  • Analyze usage for service and website improvement

Legal Basis: We process your data under contract (for service/account delivery), consent (for marketing), legitimate interests (service improvement and security), and, where required, with your explicit consent.

4. Marketing Communications

We only send marketing communications (news, updates, offers) if you have given explicit consent (e.g., by ticking a checkbox at sign-up or in your profile).
You may withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Clicking “unsubscribe” in any marketing email

We respect and honor opt-out requests globally.

Your data may be shared with:

We never sell or rent your personal data.

6. International Transfers

We may process your data outside your home country (including the U.S. and EEA). When we do, we use safeguards required by GDPR, CCPA, and other local laws, such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

To exercise your rights, email us at ds.property.management.florida@gmail.com or contact us below.
