Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 26 June 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how DSP Group and its subsidiaries— DS Property Maintenance, Border 2 Border, and Splash Zone Pools (“we”, “our”, “us”) use cookies and similar technologies on our websites and applications.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work, remember your preferences, and collect analytics to improve services.

What Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies — Essential for the website to function:
sessionid (Django): Keeps you logged in.
csrftoken (Django): For security, prevents CSRF attacks.
messages (Django): Stores temporary notification messages.
Payment & Security Cookies — Set by Stripe for secure payment processing.
Functionality & Preferences Cookies — Remembers preferences if you use those features.
Marketing & Analytics Cookies — At this time, we do not use cookies for analytics, marketing, or social media tracking that would require opt-in consent under EU/UK law.

How We Use Cookies

• To keep you logged in and secure on your account
• For payments and security
• To remember your settings (if any)

Cookie Consent Banner

We do not display a cookie consent banner or pop-up because our site does not target EU/UK users and does not use non-essential, marketing, or analytics cookies that require consent under GDPR or UK law. Should we add such cookies in the future or begin marketing in those regions, we will update our policies and site features to include an opt-in banner.

How to Control Cookies

• You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being set.
• Disabling cookies may affect some site features (for example, you may not be able to log in).

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third parties (like Stripe, if you pay online). We do not control these cookies directly. You can review their policies on their respective sites.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted here.
