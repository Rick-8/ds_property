About Us

Border 2 Border offers premium property maintenance services designed to enhance both form and function of your home or garden. From landscaping to bespoke improvements, we tailor every solution to your needs.

We focus on quality and detail, delivering results that are both reliable and visually impressive.

What We Do

Landscaping

Custom garden design and outdoor transformations to match your vision and lifestyle.

Grounds Maintenance

Comprehensive upkeep of lawns, hedges, and outdoor areas to ensure your property stays clean, safe, and visually impressive all year round.

Property Repairs & Upkeep

Interior and exterior maintenance including gutter cleaning, minor repairs, fence restoration, and painting.

Garden Clearances

We clear overgrown gardens, storm debris, sheds, and green waste — with eco-conscious disposal practices.

Fencing & Gates

Professional installation of timber or composite fencing and secure gates to enhance your property's boundary.

Jet Washing

We restore driveways, patios, and walls with high-pressure cleaning for a fresh, renewed look.

Decking & Timber Work

Install or restore decking, pergolas, planters, and timber features that elevate your outdoor space.

Seasonal Services

From winter gritting to spring planting and summer tidying — we keep your outdoor spaces season-ready.

Stump Removal & Turf Laying

Efficient tree stump grinding and complete removal, followed by professional-grade turf installation to restore vibrant, level lawn spaces with instant impact.
